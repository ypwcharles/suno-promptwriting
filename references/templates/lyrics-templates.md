## Section template (with meta tag stacking)

Meta tags belong in **Lyrics**, placed at the start of each section:

```text
///*****///

[Verse 1 | <vocal tag> | <energy tag> | <instrumental tag>]
<line 1>
<line 2>
<line 3>
<line 4>

[Chorus 1 | anthemic chorus | stacked harmonies | high energy]
<line 1>
<line 2>
<line 3>
<line 4>
```

## Performance cues

- Use parentheses for background vocals: `(fading away...)`, `(RISE UP NOW!)`.
- Use capitalization to imply intensity (ALL CAPS = loud/intense; sentence case = calm/quiet).
- Separate sections with blank lines.
